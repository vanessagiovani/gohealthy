Water Intake


Did you know that up to 60% of the human body is water? For your body to function properly, it needs to consume enough water. GoHealthy provides a feature for you to adjust your drinking target and schedule. We will send you daily reminders to drink water.


Diet


The human body needs enough nutrients such as carbohydrate, protein, fat, minerals, fibre, and vitamins. Using GoHealthy, you can check the nutrient details for your daily consumption, as well as receive food suggestions.
